The above error seems to be caused by /etc/hostname being used by initrd when nfs-mounting the root on startup.
Centos 7: /etc/hostname
Centos 6:
vi /etc/sysconfig/network
NETWORKING=yes
HOSTNAME=localhost.localdomain
